Research Data Instruction, Consulations and Presentations

I provide individual consultations, classes and workshops in research processes. I can help you with locating research materials, in creating systematic reviews. My colleagues and I provide classes on Git Bash (terminal commands), Github, Jupyter Notebooks, Python, R, and OpenRefine. We also assist in geodata and supporting ARCGIS. I can help also assist you in data visualization, data management, and selecting data repositories

Provide Support for Data and Research Activities

I also investigate and report on research best practices. I support copyright and licensing activities, scholarly reputation management, and open science - in particular open data and software

Participate on Data Initiatives on Campus and Beyond

I bring my experience to bear in campus grants and large scale discussions on appropriate data activities on the University of Arkansas Campus and throughout the state of Arkansas.
